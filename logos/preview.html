Tech Marketing Dose — Logo Options

6 concepts exploring different visual metaphors for "dose" in AI marketing

Option 1 — The AI Pill

Concept: A capsule/pill icon with AI circuit nodes inside — the literal "dose" metaphor. Friendly, immediately communicates the brand's value prop of AI knowledge delivered in digestible form.

Option 2 — The Droplet

Concept: A liquid drop with an AI brain pattern — suggesting a concentrated dose of intelligence. More abstract and premium-feeling. "DOSE" dominates as the hero word with "Tech Marketing" as a descriptor above.

Option 3 — The Bolt

Concept: A lightning bolt that doubles as a "D" inside a rounded dark square — energy, speed, daily delivery. The separate "AI" badge makes the topic explicit. Bold, app-icon-ready, works great at small sizes.

Option 4 — The Prescription

Concept: A stylized Rx symbol with circuit-node accents — "your daily AI news prescription." Clever, differentiated, positions the content as essential rather than optional. The serif Rx contrasts with the modern sans-serif wordmark.

Option 5 — The Signal

Concept: A pulse/signal wave terminating in an AI node — the "pulse" of the marketing world delivered as a dose. Clean, modern, editorial. The gradient underline adds energy. Stacked layout works for both header and social avatar use.

Option 6 — The Monogram

Concept: A "TMD" monogram in a dark circle with signal arcs radiating outward — broadcasting intelligence. The red "D" pops as the key brand element. Three colored dots at baseline represent the three content types (news, tools, strategy).
